FARMVILLE ARTICLE TITLE

Things to do whilst you wait for the timers to finish If you’re like us and you’re on this site that means you enjoy playing Farmville just like us, and as much as fun as Farmville is the frustration of having to wait for a plant to harvested is frustrating. That’s why we have come up with a list of 5 things to do whilst waiting for the timers on your crops and animals to end.

1. USE THE TOILET - More than likely you haven’t just been playing Farmville, you’ve been refreshing your Facebook, Twitter & Instagram timelines. After a while of refreshing timelines and playing Farmville you realise that you have been sitting down for almost 2 hours, not that bad but when you have a crop that takes 5 minutes to harvest taking that 5-minute bathroom break isn’t so bad.

2. DO WORK – Just because your Farmville world is frozen temporarily it doesn’t mean you can neglect your other responsibilities, go do that work that you have been holding off for so long.

3. WATCH A SERIES – Nothing better to pass time on a new series that people have been talking about but you haven’t been bothered to start yet but guess what? This is the perfect time!

4. EAT – Prepare some food, whether it be something small like cereal or a nice hearty roast dinner fill that void in your stomach that has been there whilst you play Farmville

5. BROWSE THE OUR SITE – Take a look at what the site has to offer, visit our community page and see what everyone else is up to or just have a look at the how to play page to see what tips or tricks you could get.
