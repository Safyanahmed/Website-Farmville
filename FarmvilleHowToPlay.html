HOW TO PLAY FARMVILLE

Farmville is all about creating the most successful farm possible. Here are some how-to’s and tips to start you off.

First things first, even though it’s tempting to go in and try to create the biggest farm possible, it is better to take it slowly. Familiarise yourself with the interface and what crops give you the best xp/profit. Perhaps start off with Double Grain which give you 3 xp and 104 profit within 2 hours or Long Onion for 4 xp and 235 profit inside 12 hours if you have a little more time. One thing to keep in mind is that the growing time is the same as the harvest time. For example Raspberries will take 2 hours to grow, so need to be harvested within 2 hours after fully grown otherwise they will wither.

Once you have enough coins expand your farm with plots, remember to keep it organised to maximise efficiency. If there is no more room for plots then you’ll have to buy more land. Next start to buy animals that can offer new items such as milk, eggs, and wool that you can sell for some extra coins. Levelling up can also give you a tidy sum of coins and occasionally some more xp as a reward. The more you level up the more crops and new items can be unlocked. Eventually you’ll have enough coins to buy Animal Pens that can offer your farm new ways to store animals like Chicken coops and stables.


  • Tip: Instead of buying animals at the start, add friends that can send the same Gifts to you. Remember to send them some back once you’re up and running.

  • Tip: Buy vehicles to help you plow, plant, and harvest crops in no time.

  • Tip: Fruits from trees do not wither so you can harvest them whenever you want.

  • Tip: Complete achievements to gain more coins and xp.

  • Tip: Animal Pens allow you to harvest animal items in one go.

    ITEMS IN THE GAME (via Market) (FarmVille 1)

    Item - Seeds

    Purchase a range of different seeds from the market to plant and harvest crops in order to sell.

    Item - Aninmal

    Buying animals from the market offer different items that can be collected, such as milk, eggs, and wool.

    Item - Decorations

    Decorations can make your farm more attractive. You can buy decorations from market or be gifted to you by a friend.

    Item - Buildings

    Buildings can offer a lot to a farm. They can brought as storage for animals, crafting, and decorations.

    Item - Farm Aides

    Farm Aids are used in different farm processes. For example the Tractor is used as a catalyst/helper when harvesting crops. Farm Aids are the tools in which assist you when working on the farm.

    Item - Trees

    Trees can produce rewards like coins, coconuts, and cash when they are ready to be harvested. 16 trees can be planted on one plot.

    Item - Clothing

    Clothing is used to dress your avatars. They are available to level 1 farmers upwards

    Item - Land Expansion

    Land expansions increase your farms. Expansions are always added to the bottom sides of the farm. They can be purchased from farm aides in the market.
