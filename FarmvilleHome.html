ABOUT FARMVILLE

Play the most successful and exciting free-to-play farming simulation there is. Farmville will have you building a successful working farm. Design your farm the way you want. The bigger the better! Grow and harvest crops such as strawberries, raspberries, and apples, use decorations to make the coolest looking farm there is, raise animals that give you produce, and construct buildings so that you can grow your farm into the best there is. Level up your avatar to unlock new items from the ever expanding marketplace, a place where all your farming needs will be fulfilled. You want it? They have it.

The fun doesn’t just stop there, through Facebook’s social network capabilities show off your farm to others, trade with neighbours, play with friends by visiting their farms, swap items, and help each other maintain both of your farms in order to receive in-game cash which can be used to buy the best and sometimes special limited edition items for your farm.

Not around your desktop PC? No worries, you can play on a variety of different platforms such as Facebook, iPhone, Android, and tablets so you will never be too far from your farm.
